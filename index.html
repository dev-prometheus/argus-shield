Protection

What Argus detects

Real-time analysis of signature requests to identify the attack patterns used by modern wallet drainers.

Permit2 Signatures

Detects PermitBatchTransferFrom and PermitTransferFrom — the signatures that let attackers drain multiple tokens at once.

EIP-2612 Permits

Catches native token permit signatures that work even on fresh wallets with no prior approval transactions.

Approval Analysis

Warns when approving tokens to Permit2 or unknown contracts that could set up future attacks.

Blind Sign Block

Automatically blocks eth_sign requests that can sign arbitrary data without revealing contents.

Whitelist System

Pre-configured with trusted DeFi protocols. Add your own verified addresses to reduce noise.

Activity Log

Track every intercepted request with detailed logs. Know exactly what threats were blocked.

Process

How it works

Seamless protection that runs in the background without affecting your Web3 experience.

1

Intercept

Hooks into your wallet provider and catches all signature requests before they execute.

2

Analyze

Parses the request to detect Permit2, native permits, and dangerous approval patterns.

3

Assess

Classifies risk as Critical, High, or Medium based on potential asset impact.

4

Decide

Shows you the threat details. You choose to block or proceed.
